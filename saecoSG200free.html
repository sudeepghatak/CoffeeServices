    SAECO SG200

    DIMENSIONS:

    1567 (H) *410 (W)* 537(D)

    DESCRIPTION:

    Saeco sg 200 is a real beans, granulated milk machine available only in refurbished condition. The biggest advantages have to be the extensive list of drink options; it’s safe to say that all your office coffee needs will be met – barring the connoisseur who drinks nothing but fresh milk type drinks, as this machine makes use solely of powdered milk. Available on a monthly zero rental scheme or a low weekly rental.

    FEATURES:

    • Removable brewing group
    • Painted, zinc-plated sheet body
    • Transparent coffee bean hopper – food-contact approved polypropylene
    • Air-break device and anti-overflow valve and anti-flooding security solenoid valve
    • Easy cleaning and low maintenance
    • Uses granulated vending milk powder
    • Self-explanatory operation, customer friendly interface with no assistance required
    • Optional coin mechanism

    SELECTIONS:

    Cappuccino, Long Black, Espresso, Chai latte ,Hot Chocolate, Mochaccino,Café Latte,Flat White, Milk, Plain tea or Black tea or Ginger Tea, Coffee with Milk ( Can have 7-8 possible Coffee selections and 1 tea selection)
