    COLLIBRI

    DIMENSIONS:

    1560 ML(H) *410 ML(W)* 490ML(D)

    DESCRIPTION:

    Collibri is a basic real beans, granulated milk machine available only in refurbished condition. The Colibri is a bean to cup machine that only occupies a small footprint making it perfect for even the tightest office or workplace locations. Enjoy the experience of real continental drinks such as espresso and cappuccino directly from the coffee bean to your cup. Ideal for offices and small environments and available on free rental scheme (Conditions applied) or a nominal weekly rental.

    FEATURES:

    • Removable brewing group
    • Transparent coffee bean hopper – food-contact approved polypropylene
    • Easy loading and cleaning
    • Base and coin mechanism optional
    • Ideal for small to medium sized workplaces

    SELECTIONS:

    Cappuccino, Long Black, Espresso,Hot Chocolate, Mochaccino,Café Latte,Flat White, Milk, Coffee with Milk ( Can have 6-7 possible Coffee selections)
